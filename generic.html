QR Rush

Dive into the world of QR Rush, an interactive mobile game that takes the essence of Pokémon Go and gives it a modern twist! Instead of chasing down Pokémon, players scan QR codes hidden across real-world locations. Each code scanned can be uploaded in real time, and players can drop pins on a shared, interactive map for all other users to see. The adventure doesn't stop there—scan the pins others leave behind and see what you find! Every QR code you collect appears on your personal profile, each with its own rarity. The rarer the code, the more points you earn. Compete with friends and rivals alike as you climb the leaderboard and have the ultimate QR Rush!

How It Works:

Behind the scenes, I leveraged Firebase to store user data securely and ensure seamless connectivity for all players. Firebase's real-time database enables the interactive map to stay up-to-date with every new QR scan and pin drop. The leaderboard is also dynamically updated, keeping players in sync and competition fierce! For the map functionality, I integrated Google Maps API, which allows players to navigate and discover QR code hotspots with ease. This Android application was built in Android Studio using Java, bringing together the interactive gaming experience in a fluid and engaging interface.
